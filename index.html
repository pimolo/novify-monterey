Monterey
Pop Music Festival was a three-day concert event held in 1967 in Monterey, California.
The artists performed for free with all revenue donated to charity, except for Ravi Shankar.
The festival is remembered for the first major appearances by Jimi Hendrix, The Who and Ravi Shankar.
